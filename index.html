Welcome to Corps Esprit

Experience Well-Being

Corps Esprit is your wellness center situated in Gatineau. Our rapidly growing buisness is devided in two health care domains. The Studio contains two beautiful set up yoga rooms that are perfect to host all of our classes. Our Clinic is made up of three professional rooms where our team of certified experts can escess your needs and help you achice and healthier and happier lifestyle. Between our Studio and our Clinic, we offer a wide range of services like; yoga, tai-chi, pilates, classes for mothers and their newborns, massage therapy, osteopathy and many other courses that can help you maintain your health and well being. Our mission is to offer you a warm experience along the route to living in harmony between mind and body.

The Studio

If your looking to get back into shape, to stay in shape, or simply get out of the house for a few hours to do something fun, Corps Esprit has a class for you! Our sunlit rooms are the perfect embiance to bring warmth and piece of mind to your body. In our Studio, you can participate in classes like: yoga (hot, flow, power) pilates, class for expecting mothers, mothers with babies, you can even join us for some Tai-Chi!

Our Clinic

If your feeling pain in your body that is preventing you from living your life to the fullest. Our clinic and fix you up and help you get back on the path to happiness. Our Clinic is composed of 3 rooms where our team of certified experts can help you achieve a happier and healthier lifestyle. At our Clinic we offer massage therapy and osteopathy.
